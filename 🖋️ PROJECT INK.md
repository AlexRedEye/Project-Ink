## ğŸ° Character Collection (Offline Gacha)

Character acquisition uses **gacha-style RNG** strictly for **collection thrill and anticipation**.

### Core Principles

- **Completely free**
    
- No real-money purchases
    
- Earned via gameplay:
    
    - quests
        
    - challenges
        
    - bosses
        
- **Weekly acquisition cadence**
    

### Gacha Pacing

- Players must **actively earn gacha currency**
    
- Weekly limits prevent rapid exhaustion of the roster
    
- A well-engaged player can unlock **~5 characters per week**
    
- Progression is intentionally slowed to:
    
    - preserve excitement
        
    - avoid burnout
        
    - make characters feel special
        

The system is designed to be **rewarding, not tedious**.

## ğŸ§° Gear & Equipment System

Project Ink includes a **light but meaningful gear system** to enhance strategy.

### Gear Acquisition

- Gear is earned through:
    
    - bosses
        
    - dungeon-style combat encounters
        
- Gear has **rarity tiers**
    

### Targeted Farming Philosophy

- Each farming location drops **only two gear sets**
    
- This reduces frustration and RNG overload
    
- Players can intentionally farm toward specific builds

## ğŸ“ˆ Character Progression & Training
