# RPS Advantage System

## Types
- FORCE
- GUARD
- SKILL

## Relationship
FORCE > GUARD > SKILL > FORCE

## Modifiers (MVP)
- Effective: 1.25x
- Neutral: 1.00x
- Resisted: 0.75x

## Where it applies
- Damage scaling (see [[10 - Combat/Damage & Mitigation]])
- Optional: debuff/heal effectiveness later

## Notes
Keep the system *visible* in UI and logs:
- “Effective!” / “Resisted!”
- Type icons in turn header
