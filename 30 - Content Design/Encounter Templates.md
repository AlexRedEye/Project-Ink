# Encounter Templates

## Purpose
Reusable combat setups for dungeons and bosses.

---

## MVP Templates
- Balanced (1 DPS, 1 Tank, 1 Support)
- Aggressive (2 DPS, 1 Support)
- Defensive (Tank-focused)

---

## Usage
Encounters should:
- Test team composition
- Encourage adaptation
- Highlight RPS matchups
