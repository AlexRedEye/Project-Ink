# Enemy Design Rules

## Purpose
Enemies teach systems through play.

---

## Enemy Archetypes
- Glass Cannon
- Bruiser
- Disruptor
- Support Enemy

---

## MVP Enemy Rules
- Each enemy highlights one mechanic
- No enemy breaks core rules
- Bosses bend rules slightly, not break them
